## The First Thirty Seconds

Competition nerves often hit hardest before the first line. A practiced opening gives the speaker something steady to stand on while the room settles.

## What To Skip

Do not cram every note at the last minute. Choose one delivery cue, one structure cue, and one breath cue, then trust the preparation.
