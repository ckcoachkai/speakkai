## A Practical Voice Coach

Kai's work centers on helping people communicate with less pressure and more clarity. The goal is not a perfect performance; it is a speaker who understands what to practice next and why that next step matters.

## How Sessions Feel

Practice is direct, kind, and repeatable. Families and speakers leave with language they can use during everyday moments, not a pile of vague advice that disappears after the meeting ends.
